However, one critical aspect that often hinders the full potential of XR applications is the optimization of Lighting On Demand (LOD). This report aims to address the research question: "What are the optimal strategies for LOD optimization in XR?" The efficient management of lighting in XR applications is crucial, as it directly impacts user experience, system performance, and energy consumption.
